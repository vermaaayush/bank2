|| INVESTMENT ||
Our approach to responsible investing is based on three key principles:



Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) Integration

We believe that incorporating ESG factors into our investment decision-making process can lead to better long-term outcomes for our clients. We analyse a range of ESG issues relevant to each investment,including climate change, human rights, labour practices, and board diversity, among others.

Active Ownership

We believe that active engagement with the companies we invest in is an important tool to drive positive change. We engage with company management to encourage them to adopt more sustainable practices and improve their ESG performance. We also exercise our voting rights to hold companies accountable for their actions.

Impact Investing

We believe that investing in companies that are making a positive impact on society and the environment can generate attractive returns while also contributing to a more sustainable world. We actively seek out opportunities to invest in companies that are addressing global challenges such as climate change, social inequality, and access to healthcare and education.
